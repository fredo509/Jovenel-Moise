MR. Jovenel Moise

Haitian entrepreneur and politician, who served as the 43rd President of Haiti from 2017 until his assassination in 2021

Here's a time line of Mr. Jovenel's life:

  • 26 June 1968 - Born in Trou-du-Nord, Nord-Est, Haiti.
  • On February 7, 2017, he was inaugurated President of the Republic of Haiti, succeeding Jocelerme Privert.
  • In November 2017, he announced the recreation of the Armed Forces of Haiti.22, dissolved in 1995, making Haiti a country without an army.
  • In June 2020, Moïse published a decree in the official gazette replacing the penal code in force since 1836 with a new code.
  • In June 2020, Moïse published a decree in the official gazette replacing the penal code in force since 1836 with a new code. In draft since 2009, it must enter into force on June 24, 2022.
